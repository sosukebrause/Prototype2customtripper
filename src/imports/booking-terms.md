1. Booking Request and Estimated Pricing
When you click “Confirm Booking”, your request will be submitted to our team together with an estimated price range or average price generated by our system based on the details of your itinerary.
This estimate is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute a final quotation or confirmed price.
All bookings are subject to manual review and approval by our team.

2. Final Quotation
After reviewing your booking request, our team will prepare a final confirmed quotation based on the details of your itinerary.
This quotation may reflect factors including but not limited to:
Trip duration
Travel distance
Number of passengers
Stops or itinerary adjustments
Vehicle type or service level
Applicable tolls, taxes, parking, or surcharges
You will receive the final quotation together with a secure payment link to complete your booking.
Your booking is not confirmed until full payment has been received.

3. Full Payment Requirement
To confirm your reservation, full payment of the quoted amount is required at the time of checkout through the payment link.
By completing payment through the provided link, you authorize the full charge to your selected payment method for the amount shown in the final quotation.
Payments are processed securely by our payment processor, such as Stripe, and your payment details are not stored directly on our servers.

4. Refund and Cancellation Policy
Customers may cancel their booking and request a refund up to five (5) days before the first scheduled day of service.
If a cancellation is requested within this period:
The booking will be canceled.
A refund will be issued to the original payment method.
A 3.7% processing fee will be deducted from the refunded amount to cover non-refundable merchant transaction fees charged by the payment processor.
Example:
If the total payment was $1,000, the refunded amount would be $963, reflecting the 3.7% processing fee.

5. Cancellations Within Five (5) Days of Service
Cancellations made within five (5) days of the scheduled trip start date are non-refundable, as vehicles, drivers, and scheduling resources will have already been reserved and allocated for your service.

6. Changes to Bookings
If you request changes to your itinerary after payment has been made, we will make reasonable efforts to accommodate the request.
However:
Additional charges may apply for changes that increase service time, travel distance, or requested services.
If additional costs are incurred, a separate payment request may be issued.
Changes are subject to availability and operational feasibility.

7. Waiting Time Policy
Drivers will wait for passengers at the scheduled pickup location for a reasonable period of time.
Waiting time allowances may vary depending on the type of pickup location, including:
Airports
Hotels
Private residences
Other designated pickup points
If passengers do not arrive within the allotted waiting time, additional waiting charges may apply or the trip may be considered a no-show, in which case the booking may be canceled without refund.

8. Passenger Conduct and Refusal of Transportation
Refusal of Transportation or Continuation Thereof
We may refuse to accept or continue transportation in any of the following cases:
The request is not made in accordance with these Terms.
No appropriate vehicle or equipment is available for the requested transport.
The requester demands special burdens beyond standard service.
The transport would violate applicable laws, public order, or good morals.
Transportation is hindered by natural disasters or other unavoidable circumstances.
The passenger does not comply with instructions given by the driver under relevant transport regulations.
The passenger is carrying items prohibited under transport regulations.
The passenger is so intoxicated that they cannot clearly state their destination or walk without assistance.
The passenger is wearing clothing likely to excessively soil or damage the vehicle.
The passenger is seriously ill and unaccompanied.
The passenger is suspected to have a Class I, Class II, or designated infectious disease requiring hospitalization under applicable infectious disease control laws.

9. Smoking Ban
In vehicles designated as non-smoking vehicles, passengers must refrain from smoking.
If a passenger smokes or attempts to smoke in a non-smoking vehicle, the driver may request that the passenger stop immediately. If the passenger refuses to comply, transportation may be refused or discontinued.

food waste
mud or sand
smoking
bodily fluids
other conditions requiring professional cleaning
Any such charges may be billed to the customer responsible for the booking.
